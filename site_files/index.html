Welcome! This web archive contains 6000 images of New Vision Magazine, published in Uganda in the 2000s and 2010s. Dr. Caroline Faria from the Department of Geography and the Environment at UT uses these images as data for her NSF project on the beauty and fashion industry in Uganda. She has made these images available on this web site to university students and faculty for their own research. Feel free to browse the web archive for images, or check explore the site to learn more about Dr. Faria, her research, and UT's Feminist Geography Collective.
